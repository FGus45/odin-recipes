Grilled Chicken

This grilled chicken recipe delivers juicy, flavorful chicken with a smoky, charred finish. Marinated in a blend of olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and herbs, the chicken absorbs vibrant Mediterranean flavors that perfectly complement its natural richness. The marinade helps tenderize the meat while adding a bright, tangy taste that balances the char from the grill.

Whether cooked over charcoal for extra smokiness or on a gas grill for convenience, the chicken develops a golden, crispy exterior while remaining moist and tender inside. Ideal for outdoor cookouts or a quick, healthy weeknight dinner, this grilled chicken pairs well with fresh salads, roasted vegetables, or a side of rice.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (or thighs)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice (about 1 lemon)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano (or 1 tablespoon fresh)
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
